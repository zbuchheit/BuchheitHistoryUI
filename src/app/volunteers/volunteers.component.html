Would you like to volunteer?

One way for this website to remain a valuable resource is to continue adding to it!

We are always looking for a few good volunteers . . . If you would be interested in contributing to this website, by sharing BUCHHEIT related documents you may have gathered, please feel free to contact Julie Reese Bookser.

   How Can YOU Help?

  • There are four categories in the left hand list of documents requesting help from researchers and volunteers. Three are News - HELP?, Obituary - HELP? and Pictures - HELP?. If you are familiar with the documents found in these categories and can help place them into a branch of the BUCHHEIT family, please send an email to the Webmaster so the information can be added to the database.

  • The fourth section where your help is needed is the Census-HELP? category. If you are familiar with the families in any of these census records and can help place them into a branch of the BUCHHEIT family, please send an email to the Webmaster so the information can be added to the database. Census records are a great way to help connect the branches of the Buchheit tree, so identifying the members of the census records is very important.

  • On the right hand column, there are three links that are in need of help from researchers and volunteers. Are you familiar with a cemetery, library or other resource that would be able to provide a BUCHHEIT researcher with information about their branch of the family tree? If you do, please forward that information to the Webmaster so it can be added to this website.

  • We are also looking for a volunteer who will type the contents of scanned newspaper obituaries and save them in a .txt format. If you are interested in volunteering for this task, please contact the Webmaster. Thanks!

  • Would anyone be interested in helping us find the contact information and/or websites for some of the cemeteries listed on the Cemetery Resource page? If you are interested in volunteering for this task, please contact the Webmaster. Thanks!

  • Would anyone be interested in helping us find the information and/or websites for some of the libraries listed on the Library Resource page? Do you know of any other libraries that should be added to this page? If you are interested in volunteering for this task, please contact the Webmaster. Thanks!

  • Would anyone be interested in helping us find the information and/or websites for resources to be listed on the Resources page? If you are interested in volunteering for this task, please contact the Webmaster. Thanks!

  • Does anyone know of a personal website that contains information about the Buchheit family that can be listed on the Web Resources page? If you are interested in volunteering for this task, please contact the Webmaster. Thanks!
